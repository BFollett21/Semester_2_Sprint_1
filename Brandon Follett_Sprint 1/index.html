Our Story


Our story startes all the way back in kindergarten. Me and my best friend whose name rhymes with Very Frown, both had a dream of setting out and starting our very own chicken restaurant. But, as we got older we grew apart and she started her own business without me. So one day I said to myself "Why don't we have a little friendly fried chicken competition?" and thats when Gary Blue's was born. As of today we have as many locations across the province as my good friend Very Frown, and because of this there are no losers in our competition and the only winners are you, the customer.


Locations


You can find a Gary Blue's Diner conveniently and coincidentally directly across the street from each and every one of our competitors locations. (You know, the one that rhymes with Very Frown's).


Hours of Operation


All of our locations are open from 9:30 a.m. - 1:00 a.m. So you can always feel the Blue's. (Conveniently and coincidentally open a full hour earlier and we don't close for a whole hour later than our competition, who rhymes with Very Frown's.)
